Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. KHIPUAI is a sole proprietorship (Wilfredo Mora) based in Ontario, Canada, serving clients across the United States and Canada.

What this covers

This policy explains what information KHIPUAI collects through this website, how it is used, and your choices. It is written in plain language and kept deliberately short.

What we collect

  • Information you give us. If you book a call or request the self-assessment, we collect the details you provide (typically your name, email, company, and anything you write to us).
  • Basic usage data. We use privacy-friendly website analytics to understand aggregate traffic (for example, page views and referring source). We do not use this to identify you personally.

How we use it

  • To send you the resource you requested (such as the self-assessment).
  • To respond to you, schedule a call, and follow up about the AI Automation Audit.
  • To understand, in aggregate, which content and channels are useful.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

Who processes your data

We use reputable third-party tools to run this site and our outreach (for example, a scheduling tool, an email or form provider, and website analytics). Each processes data only to provide its service. If you book a call or opt in, your details are stored in those tools and in our own contact records.

Confidentiality of client work

Any business information a client shares during an engagement is treated as confidential and is never published or shared without written permission. Case examples on this site are anonymized.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or unsubscribe from follow-ups, at any time. Email wil@khipuai.co and we will take care of it.

Data security

We use least-privilege access and reputable, secured services. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.
